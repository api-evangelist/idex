# IDEX Corporation (idex)
IDEX Corporation (NYSE: IEX) is a global engineered products company that designs and manufactures pumps, flow meters, valves, fluidics systems, precision components, and fire and safety equipment. IDEX serves health, science, water, energy, semiconductor, aerospace, fire and safety, and industrial markets through three segments: Fluid and Metering Technologies, Health and Science Technologies, and Fire and Safety / Diversified Products.
